About New Institue Hawaii

New Insitute is a subsidiary of the Ohana Foundation for Technological Development. We partner with leading Institutes to provide state-of-the-art leadership training to future leaders in politics, governments, public sectors, non-profits and educaiton in America, Asia and across the world.

Leaders For Tomorrow

At our headquarters in Nauru Tower in Honolulu, we welcome people of all ages to learn to succeed in politics and effect real change. New Institute provides a place for like-minded people to hold meetings, events, speeches, and organize.

Our Vision

We are dedicated to training and mentoring the next generation to make effective policy change, restore balance in our communities, and implement innovative solutions to real world issues.
